About MAHA

Maha is a brand born out of love for ceramics,
vivid colours and passion for life.

Abdullah, Ali & Haseeb

Three creative minds that came together at art school, Ali and Haseeb founded Maha with a shared passion for design and sustainability. For them, success is more than just about having a bestselling product.

In MAHA MA stands for Muhammad Abdullah and AH for Ali Hasan and lastly HA stands for Haseeb Attari. They take inspiration from the world around them when it comes to shapes, colors, and the clay that they work with.
